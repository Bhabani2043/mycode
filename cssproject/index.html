Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is an optimistic, carefree pirate with the dream of becoming the Pirate King. He possesses the powers of the Gum-Gum Fruit, which makes his body stretch like rubber.

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro is a skilled swordsman and loyal member of the Straw Hat Pirates, known for his determination and strength. He wields three swords, using his unique "Santoryu" style.

Shanks

Shanks is a charismatic and powerful pirate, former mentor to Luffy, and captain of the Red-Haired Pirates. Known for his calm demeanor and strength, he played a pivotal role in inspiring Luffy's journey to become Pirate King.

Sanji

Sanji is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates, known for his incredible martial arts skills using only his legs. He is a chivalrous and flirtatious gentleman.
